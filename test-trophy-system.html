🏆 Trophy System & Restart Updates

Updated the results panel with a new trophy ranking system and improved restart functionality.

✨ Changes Made:

1. Enhanced Restart Text:
Changed from "Press R to restart game" to "Press R or click anywhere to restart game"
2. Colored Trophy System:
Different trophies and colors based on player ranking (see examples below)
3. Click-Anywhere Restart:
The entire results panel is now clickable for restarting, not just the text
